Patient Empowerment

Patient involvement permits patient to certify data collected and entered by the Health Care Professional (stored on a HIPAA secure server).

SafeStart is a modern and convenient tablet application that streamlines surgical preparation

SafeStarts's patient portal allows patients to confirm or request correction of their information prior to surgery.

How it works

Enter Patient Data

During the pre-procedure interview, the HCP enters the patient’s information into the appropriate fields (name, sex, DOB, allergies, etc).

This information will be used to generate the SafeStart timeout checklist at the time of procedure.

The HCP has the option to request patient verification by email.

Create Patient Account

The patient receives an email with a link to SafeStarts patient portal. The patient securely creates an account and verifies their identity by entering a birthdate.

Verify or Reject Patient Data

Patients review the information entered by their HCP to ensure accuracy, and either accept or reject the information. SafeStart notifies the HCP by email of patient verification or patient rejection of the information.

Advance To Timeout

When the surgery team is ready to begin the Timeout checklist, the HCP presses “Advance to Timeout” and is prompted to take a photo of prepped, draped and marked surgical site.

SafeStart merges the previously entered patient information with the new photograph to create a patient specific checklist.

Timeout Checklist

The HCP may edit allergies and then complete the checklist item entries on the iPad.

Timeout Checklist

The user completing the checklist enters their name in a text field and signs their name on the iPad.

The completed checklist can be wirelessly printed, saved to any server or attached electronically to an available HL-7 EMR.
